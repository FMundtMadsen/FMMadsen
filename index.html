About

I am a molecular biologist with nearly two decades of experience studying how life works at the molecular level.

I received my PhD in medical science from the Karolinska Institute and have conducted research at institutions including the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard, and the University of Copenhagen.

I currently work in translational medicine, connecting molecular understanding to real-world biology and human health.

Biology is not as complicated as it first appears.
